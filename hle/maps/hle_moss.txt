Name:		Moss
Game:		Half-Life Elite Death Match
Author:	SantagausS

Moss for Half-Life Elite is a Medium to large sized map that 
will cater to a wide range of player styles and tactics.  It 
has a fairly even spread of weapons through out the map. 
Player spawn in points are also spread evenly through the map.  
The map is generously stocked with weapons and ammo. This map 
should have a fairly flexible set of successful tactics. Small 
jumps and technical movement are important in this map.
