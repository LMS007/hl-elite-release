Name:		Stalk Yard Capture The Flag 
Game:		Half-Life Elite Capture The Flag
Author:	SantagausS

A capture the flag version of the valve released map stalkyard. 
This map pits Red and Blue teams against each other. The main 
outside yard is the center of each teams play, farther back 
through two halls are the flags in another outside area.  
Collect your supplies fast or the game will outrun you.  
Jumping on boxes and technical movement are an extreme 
advantage in this map.
